# SiSy Demo Script

> Sisy is a routine management app inspired by the **"Getting Things Done"** methodology and the 4 Laws of Behavior Change from the book **"Atomic Habits"**.  

> You can start with sharing your profile or your existing routine, you can do it via app's profile and routine pages or via describing or copying your profile or routine to the chat box.

## 1 scene:
On the me page, input following profile, and show the updated me page.

Example profile:
```
I'm Sarah, 35, software engineer. I love reading & hiking. Been feeling kinda overwhelmed lately with work, want to start a better morning routine to get my day going with more energy. I have iron deficiency
```

> On the Routine Page, AI updated items will be highlighted. 
Routine items can be set to auto-complete,  They are  displayed in a lighter color, and they will disappear from the present page when the time comes. This is to let the user focus on the important tasks.


## 2 scene:
On the routine page, input following text to the chat field, and show the updated routine page

Example routine:
```
I wake up at 7am, brush teeth and wash face. Then I drink a glass of water. Around 7:30 I go for a 30 min jog. Shower at 8:15. Breakfast at 8:45, usually oatmeal. Start work at 9:30.
```

>  On the minimalist Present Page, the user is guided to focus on the current task. Swipe right to complete a task, or swipe left to reschedule it. Click the task to view the details or add a comment. The details could include a concrete step by step plan to complete the task. This is a great help to forge a new good habit.

## 3 scene:

On the Present Page, swipe right to complete a task, and then swipe left to reschedule another task. Then click the task show the task details. 


>  You can also share your feelings and difficulties via chatting with SiSy. Sisy will try to provide helpful suggestions based on your profile and the current situatio.

## 4 scene:

In the Chat interface, type "food ideas?"

Then type I like spinach, update my preferences.

There is a short cut to ask Sisy to refine the routine item. Sisy will make personalized suggestions to make the routine item more actionable.

On the Me Page, a flexible user profile is maintained in key-value pairs. The Profile and Routine pages contain all the data that SiSy knows about the user.

SiSy is built for the user who values their time and energy, it provides the structure to iterate and improve over time.
